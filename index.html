Spencer's Ski Tour Guide

Welcome! This is where I'm going to spill the beans on some of my favorite tours here in the Wasatch. If you want world-class skiing, go to the Cottonwoods like everyone else. If you enjoy a bit more solitude and adventure, however, then consider the mountains along the Wasatch front from about as far north as Thurstan Peak to as far south as Grandview Peak. I haven't skied everything in this range, but I have explored enough of it to have something to say about it. It is a bit foolish to share this knowledge (rather than keep it secret), but the mountains are right there for everyone to plainly see as they drive down the freeway. That, and I can't help contain my enthusiasm for the subject.

What follows is a list of tours that I enjoy in the said area, each presented as a written description with pictures and downloadable KML files. You can use Google Earth to load the KML files, or the desktop version. Either one.

  • Francis Peak
  • Mud Peak
  • Kenny Peak
  • Sessions Peak
  • Bountiful Ridge
  • Grandview Peak
